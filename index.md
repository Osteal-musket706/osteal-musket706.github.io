Putting notes on the staff requires three basic steps.

1. Choose the "Note Input" tab at the top of the window.
2. Select the duration of the note you want to write, such as a quarter note or eighth note.
3. Click on the staff lines to place the note at the pitch you want.

If you make a mistake, press "Delete" on your keyboard to remove the selected note. To change a note, click it and drag it up or down to adjust its pitch.

You will likely perform these tasks often as you write music.
